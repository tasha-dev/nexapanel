# 🚀 NexaPanel

A modern SaaS-style admin dashboard built with **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **React Query**.

NexaPanel is a frontend-focused project that demonstrates real-world dashboard architecture, API integration, CRUD operations, reusable components, and responsive UI design using a mock backend.

---

## ✨ Features

- Authentication
- Product and their management
- Carts and their management
- Users and their management
- Recepies and their management
- Posts and their management
- Comments and their management
- Todos and their management
- Quotes and their management
